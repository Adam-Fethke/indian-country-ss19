Ah- Tah- Thi- Ki Muuseum

In the Seminole language, Ah-Tah-Thi-Ki means a 'place to learn'. The goal of this museum is to preserve the Seminole culture for the present and future generations. A lot of there collections and items are based on based on culture from the Southeastern Native American populations according to their official website. There are concerns that the surrounding gaming economy could have affected the culture of this tribe.
